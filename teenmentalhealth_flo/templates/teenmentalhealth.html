Teen Mental Health Problems

Teen Mental Health Problems are at an all time high, even being considered an "epidemic" by the American Academy of Pediatrics along with the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry and the Children's Hospital Association

Our Goal

Our goal is to create a safe environment for teens to anonymously talk to their peers about there mental health. This can be how they're feeling, problems they are having, how they can fix problems, etc.

Problem

Teens can sometimes feel more conforatable talking to a peer then an adult, our goal is to just create an environment to safely chat with peers. We would still reccomend speaking with an adult if you have bad thoughts, but talking with a peer can help with feeling better, confidence, and just getting things off your chest and talking can be nice.
